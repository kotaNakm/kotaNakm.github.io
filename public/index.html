My research focuses on large-scale, dynamic, and multi-aspect data, with the goal of designing scalable, adaptive, and relational machine learning systems, thereby extending their applicability across a broader range of complex, real-world scenarios, particularly:

  • Mining Complex Time-stamped Event Streams
  • Predicting Future Events by Discovering Predictive Patterns
  • Time Series Transformation for Ultrafast Arriving Data Streams

Keywords: Data Stream Mining, Tensor Analysis, Probabilistic Generative Model, Time Series Analysis, Information Theory for Model Selection, Multi-objective Optimization.
